Ice Cream Cheesecake Cake Recipe

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon brown sugar
  • 7 Tablespoons butter, melted

Cheesecake Layer:

  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ⅔ cups sour cream
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature, lightly beaten

Ice Cream Layer:

  • 1 ½ quarts vanilla ice cream (or any preferred flavor), slightly softened

Optional Toppings:

  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate or caramel drizzle
  • Fresh berries
  • Crushed cookies

Instructions

Cheesecake Layer:

  1. Preheat oven to 325F (160C).
  2. Prepare graham cracker crust by combining graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and brown sugar. Stir well. Add melted butter and mix with a fork until well combined.
  3. Press the crust mixture firmly into the bottom and slightly up the sides of a 9” springform pan. Set aside.
  4. In a stand mixer or large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth (avoid over-beating).
  5. Add sugar and beat until creamy.
  6. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well, scraping down the sides as needed.
  7. Gradually add lightly beaten eggs, one at a time, mixing on low until just incorporated. Do not over-mix.
  8. Pour batter into prepared crust. Place pan on a foil-lined cookie sheet to catch any leaks.
  9. Bake on the center rack for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is jiggly but not liquid. Edges may be slightly puffed and golden.
  10. Remove from oven and let cool on top of the oven for 10 minutes. Run a knife around the edge to loosen.
  11. Cool at room temperature for 1–2 hours, then refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight until fully chilled and firm.

Ice Cream Layer:

  1. Once cheesecake is fully chilled, remove from springform pan and set aside.
  2. Clean the springform pan and line it with parchment paper or plastic wrap for easy removal.
  3. Spread softened ice cream evenly into the bottom of the lined springform pan. Smooth the top.
  4. Freeze for 1–2 hours, or until the ice cream is firm.
  5. Carefully place the chilled cheesecake on top of the ice cream layer inside the pan. Press gently to level.
  6. Return to the freezer and freeze at least 4 hours or overnight until cake is fully set.

To Serve:

  1. Remove cake from freezer and let sit at room temperature for 5–10 minutes before slicing.
  2. Top with whipped cream, sauce, berries, or other desired toppings. Slice and enjoy!
