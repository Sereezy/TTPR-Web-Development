My Favorite Restaurants in New York

Written by Sahil Singh

Hot Peppers

This is a fast food restaurant in Queens and Brooklyn. They serve burrito bowls. They have a wide selection of choices like steak and honey chicken.


Kebabish

This restaurant has a lot of different curries and other choices from South-Asian Countries


Barkada Social Club

This Filipino restaurant located in Astoria, Queens has a delicious selection of food ranging from lumpia to pancit to fried chicken.
