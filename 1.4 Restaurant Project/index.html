My Favorite Restaurants in New York

Written by Wen Xiong Zhang

Chinese Restaurants

This is a restaurants serving chinese food for takeout or dining in. Their mapo tofu and egg foo young are my favorite dish to eat.

99 Flavor Taste

This restaurant is essentially an all you can eat hot pot or BBQ as long as you pay $30 minimum. If it's your birthday, you can eat for free.

Barkada Social Club

This Filipino restaurant located in Astoria, Queens has a delicious selection of food ranging from lumpia to pancit to fried chicken.
