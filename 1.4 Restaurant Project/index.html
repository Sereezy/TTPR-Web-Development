My Favorite Restaurants in New York

Written by Anna Rose Cimitile

Bamontes

This is an old school Italian restaurant in the heart of Williamsburg. Best seafood dishes with a classic vibe.

Cafe Evergreen

A hole in the wall Chinese restaurant on the Upper East Side. Most delicious soup dumplings I have ever had.

Cheesecake Factory

A place that has a little bit of everything, even the bread is to die for.
