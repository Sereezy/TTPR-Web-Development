My Favorite Restaurants in New York

Written by Nadia Khan

Affy's Premium Grill

This is a halal retaurant that serves everything from steaks to a hamburger. They have pastas, steaks, pizza, burgers, and refreshing beverages.

Ayat

A casual bistro where you'll find honest, authentic Palestinian food made with love. Every plate they serve tells our story. Good Food, Real Talk, No Filter.

Barkada Social Club

This Filipino restaurant located in Astoria, Queens has a delicious selection of food ranging from lumpia to pancit to fried chicken.
