My Favorite Restaurants in New York

Written by Norm Miller

NeGril Village

This is a Jamaican oriented restaurant located in Washington Square. They authentic cuisines from the island and makes you feel as if you are just right at home.

Bibi Wine Bar

This restaurant has a grand selection of wine to choose from. Their charcuterie boards are delicious.

Barkada Social Club

This Filipino restaurant located in Astoria, Queens has a delicious selection of food ranging from lumpia to pancit to fried chicken.
