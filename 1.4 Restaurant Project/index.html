My Favorite Restaurants in New York

Written by Arisa Moonsammy

Mimisa

This is a Korean Fried Chicken spot located in Flushing. They have multiple variations of the chicken along with other Korean sides.

Olive Garden

This resturant is known for its pasta and breadsticks. (The breadsticks are yummy.)

Juju's Cafe & Deli

This is a small cafe in Downtown Brooklyn. I normally go there for their onigiri's whenever I'm up for a walk.
