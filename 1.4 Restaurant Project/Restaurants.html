My Favorite Restaurants

Written by Sherise

Outback Steakhouse

This restaurant is located in many different areas. They sell so many different foods, from steaks and mashed potatoes to chicken and fries. I love how good they steaks are.

Applebee's

Applebee's is a restaurant known for its casual dining atmosphere and a menu featuring classic American bar and grill fare, including appetizers, burgers, salads, pasta, and more.
