My favorite restaurants in New York City

Written by Sutibro Barua

Kababish

Kababish is a great restaurant known for their wonderful kebabs, it is an indian cuisine and have a wide selection of kebabs, breads and drinks.


Zyara

Zyara is known for their shawarmas and special platters, they serve chicken and beef which is marinated for days and they have various sauces to go along with the shawarmas.


Dave's hot chicken

Dave's hot chicken is known for their nashville style hot chicken.
